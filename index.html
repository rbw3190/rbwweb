The rotaract way?

Rotaract is a student organisation. It bring together people ages 18 and 30 to exchange ideas with leaders in the community, develop leadership and professional skills, and have fun through service. In communities worldwide, Rotary and Rotaract members work side by side to take action through service. It has grown into a major organization of over 10,904 clubs spread around the world and over 203,000 members in 180 countries.

Our Vision

To make Rotaract Bangalore West a platform that is wholesome and welcomes everyone with open arms; an organization that is resilient enough to face any challenges that appear on it's way.

Our Mission

The mission of RBW is to provide an opportunity for youth to enhance the knowledge and skills that will assist them in personal development, to address the social needs of their communities, and to promote better relations in the spectrum of people worldwide through a framework of friendship and service.

Get Involved!

Together, we unite the passion and diversity of Rotaractors across Bengaluru, through the empowerment of every member, the enrichment of every experience, and the collaboration of every perspective, we lift each other to be the most impactful leaders of this and every generation.
